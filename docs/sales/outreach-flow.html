Corollary // go to market

The map-as-gift outreach loop

One firm at a time. The email carries the scenario map run on the fund's own stated view, so the value arrives in the inbox before any call. Sent personally from russell@mosaiccapital.io, two to three a day, every one written by hand.

01 // ENRICH

Pick the firm and read its view

From the Eastern-time, sub-20-employee universe (targets-est.md). Read the firm's own public thesis: website, quarterly letter, a podcast or panel, and its 13F quarter-over-quarter delta for what it just got bullish on.

source: SEC Form ADV, public 13F filings, the firm's own site
Tier 1

Fresh signal

A new public statement or a sharp 13F rotation this quarter. Run the full loop now.

Tier 2

Stable view

A known strategy, no fresh signal. One letter per quarter, timed to the 13F drop.

Tier 3

No read

No public thesis to map. Skip, or park for a later signal.

02 // MAP

Run their view through the engine

Turn the stated thesis into a Corollary scenario and run it. Pull the two or three most surprising under-covered names off the second and third order nodes, with the exact filing sentence behind each. Prefer names the fund does not already hold: the gift is what they have not looked at yet.

source: Corollary scenario map, cited to SEC filings
03 // TOUCH

Send the map, not a pitch

First sentence names their specific public statement. Then the two names the second order points at, under $2B, thin coverage, claims tracing to their own 10-K sentences, with the three-node excerpt attached. Understated close: if the full map is useful, I will send a sign-in. No feature list, no deck, no hype.

voice: Atlas letter rules // frame: gift, not critique
Reply

Book the 30-minute session. Add their email to the allowlist before the call. Run their live thesis on the screen.

No reply, escalate

Follow the cadence below. Pause the moment they respond.

Escalation cadence (Mosaic rhythm)

Day 1
Email + LinkedIn. The map excerpt. Subject references their stated view, not the product.
Day 5
Email. One more node from the same map. A different under-covered name, same thesis.
Day 8
LinkedIn. Engage with their recent post. No ask.
Day 12
Email. The 30-second film link and the sign-in offer. Then stop.
Exit
No engagement, move to the Tier 2 quarterly letter, timed to the next 13F publication.

All inputs are public: Form ADV, 13F filings, published letters and interviews.
No compiled personal contact lists. Every send is personal. Nothing in any touch is investment advice.
