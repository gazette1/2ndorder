Posting lanes that fit the brand (pick one per post, never hype):
1. One real consequence chain from a public scenario, three nodes, plain
   sentences, ending with the kind of evidence the engine reads.
2. One primary-source catch, stated flatly: what a filing said versus what
   the press release led with. No tickers presented as advice.
3. One honest-limits post occasionally: what the tool does not do. This
   reads as confidence and separates the account from AI-hype finance X.

Never: engagement bait, price predictions, screenshots implying
recommendations, or replies arguing with anonymous accounts. The account is
a research journal, not a promoter.
