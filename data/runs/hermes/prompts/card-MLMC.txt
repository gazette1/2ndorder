Management s Annual Report on Internal Control over Financial Reporting. The Company s management is responsible for establishing and maintaining adequate internal control over financial reporting, as such term is defined in Rule 13a-15(f) under the Exchange Act, and in accordance with Section 404 of the Sarbanes-Oxley Act of 2002 ( Sarbanes-Oxley Act ). A material weakness is a control deficiency, or combination of control deficiencies that results in more than a remote likelihood that a material misstatement of the annual or interim consolidated financial statements will not be prevented or detected. During the assessment of the effectiveness of internal control over financial reporting as of December 31, 2015, management identified material weaknesses related to the lack of segregation of duties and the need for stronger financial reporting oversight.
