We will recognize any remaining revenue associated with a shortfall to a minimum purchase volume during the period we can assert that it is probable that a significant reversal in the amount of revenue recognized would not occur. We review our variable consideration estimates at the end of each quarter. As of December 31, 2025 and 2024, we could not assert that it was probable that a significant reversal in the amount of revenue recognized would not occur for a potential shortfall to the minimum purchase volume at the end of the in place distributor agreements, which have a remaining term of twelve months.
