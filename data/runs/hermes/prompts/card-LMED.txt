Technology Overview The Company is developing facial recognition technology ( FRT ) software that leverages artificial intelligence, machine learning, and computer vision techniques to analyze facial images and video data for identification, verification, and analytical purposes. Our development approach focuses on building software-based systems capable of operating across varied environments and data inputs. The technology is designed to capture facial images from still photographs or video streams, perform preprocessing to enhance image quality and normalize facial features, and apply analytical models to generate biometric templates that may be used for comparison and classification.
