Expansion of Manufacturing and Enrichment Capacity Update On January 5, 2026, DOE announced that American Centrifuge Operating, LLC, a subsidiary of Centrus, was selected for award of a $900.0 million task order, subject to negotiations, to expand its uranium enrichment facility in Piketon, Ohio, to include commercial-scale production of HALEU.
