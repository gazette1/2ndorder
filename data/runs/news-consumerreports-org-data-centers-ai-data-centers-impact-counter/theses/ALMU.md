## Business today
Aeluma sells heterogeneous integration of compound semiconductors on large-diameter silicon substrates for laser, high-speed datacom, and quantum applications [2][3][4]. Revenue is $4.7M and cash is $3.6M (SEC XBRL). Current contracts come from U.S. government agencies and DARPA [0][1].

## Chain position
Aeluma is a beneficiary (order 2) in the chiplet and advanced packaging adoption chain. Its platform enables hyperscalers to combine specialized chiplets (e.g., optical interconnects) on silicon, supporting the efficiency gains that keep data center electricity demand at 95GW. Revenue scales with the number of chips per server, not just server units.

Success requires converting government R&D contracts into commercial production agreements with named hyperscalers.
