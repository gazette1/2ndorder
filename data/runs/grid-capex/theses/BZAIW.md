# BZAIW: Low-power AI vendor faces headwinds from thin revenue, insider selling, and lack of commercial adoption

## Bear case first
The disconfirming reading is that rising industrial electricity costs could increase demand for Blaize’s low-power AI solutions, providing an unappreciated tailwind. However, the filings show no named enterprise customers, no design wins, and no revenue growth trajectory. The $45.8MM cash (XBRL) provides a short-term cushion, but the $38.6MM revenue (XBRL) is heavily dependent on federal awards that may not recur. Insider selling of $826K (Form 4) with zero buys signals management lack of conviction. The bear case fails because there is no evidence of commercial validation beyond government contracts.
