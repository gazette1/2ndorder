## Bear case first

The seed thesis requires a direct beneficiary of accelerated industrial adoption of VFDs, high-efficiency motors, and heat recovery systems; CTO-PA owns real estate and collects rent. Filings contain no mention of data centers, grid capex, or energy-saving technologies. Insider trading is zero, no named customers exist, and federal awards are $0. The name fails because it sits outside the adoption chain entirely.

The company has no direct or indirect exposure to the adoption curve described in the seed thesis.
